Important Information

What Device Supervision Means

Device supervision gives Altrii Recovery enhanced control over your device's restrictions. This ensures your digital wellness boundaries remain intact, even during moments of weakness.

  • Your selected restrictions become much harder to bypass
  • Profile removal requires a cooling-off period
  • Automatic security updates protect against new bypass methods
  • You maintain full control through your account
